Significant successes and achievements
Empowering

Organization of large-scale support and development programs.

Creation of an extensive database of Ukrainians living in Georgia.

Systematic research into the needs of Ukrainians for more effective planning and implementation of programs.

Opening representative offices of the organization in various regions of Georgia to expand coverage and strengthen ties with the Ukrainian diaspora.

"Unite Together" emerged from Ukrainian volunteers' desire to aid their compatriots during the war. Initially organizing charity events to send medicines to Ukraine and support Ukrainians in Georgia, it transitioned into a structured NGO after securing its first European grant following a survey of Ukrainian residents in Georgia in June 2022. Officially established as an NGO on September 1, 2022, it continues its mission to assist Ukrainians in Georgia.

Empowering Ukrainians in Georgia affected by war through sustainable systemic changes.

Unite Together was founded with the aim of providing support and development opportunities to Ukrainians in Georgia impacted by the war. Since our inception, we have been dedicated to creating projects and implementing programs for financial assistance, informal education, sports, and counseling. Our mission is to make a positive and lasting impact on the lives of those affected by the war.
