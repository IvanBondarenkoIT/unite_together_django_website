Values:

Sustainability and responsibility:

We attach great importance to creating sustainable conditions for the life and development of those affected by war. We are responsible for our obligations to beneficiaries and partners.

 

Cooperation and partnership:

We value mutually beneficial and productive cooperation with our partners and stakeholders. We believe that only through joint efforts can we achieve our goals and create positive change.

Compassion and Empathy:

In our work, we show deep compassion and empathy for everyone who needs our support and help. We listen and understand the needs of our beneficiaries, striving to provide them with not only material but also emotional support.

Changes are created here:

At the initial stage of Unite Together's activities, the organization focused on meeting the humanitarian needs of Ukrainians in Georgia. However, over time, the goals of the organization expanded, and in addition to the humanitarian mission, the task of developing and integrating Ukrainians into Georgian society was added.

Financial support for Ukrainians:

the #UkrCash program, supported by ASB, provided monthly financial aid to over 3,000 Ukrainians, totaling more than 2 million euros this year.

Help with utility bills:

launch of the #Winterization program with the support of ASB and PIN, aimed at covering utility costs for 670 families.

Support for Ukrainian communities:

reimbursement of 50% of housing costs for 90 Ukrainian families with PIN support.

Educational and cultural programs:

implementation of programs for children and the elderly in Tbilisi, Batumi, and Kutaisi, including educational events, sports, entertainment, language lessons, and master classes.

Sports initiatives:

the #UniteForActiveSports program, supported by PIN in Tbilisi and Batumi, includes sports activities like rock climbing, dancing, SUP surfing, and camping.

Support for older people:

implementation of the #HandMade program with support from CARE, including workshops, excursions and visits to cultural events such as ballet.

Medical provision:

of drugs for the treatment of thyroid diseases in Ukraine and Georgia (1260 beneficiaries)
